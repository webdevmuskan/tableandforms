Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 500g chicken
  • 2 onions
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Biryani masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak the rice.
  2. Fry onions until golden brown.
  3. Add chicken, tomatoes, and spices.
  4. Cook until chicken is tender.
  5. Boil rice separately.
  6. Layer rice and chicken mixture.
  7. Cook on low heat for 15 minutes.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
